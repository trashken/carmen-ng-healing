I was feeling run down with a sore throat from the flu, and absolutely exhausted from looking after my whole family, who had all caught the bug too. On top of that, I was hoping to receive some extra energy to take on new challenges and grow this year. After the session, I felt my whole body getting very warm. I’m not usually someone who ‘sees’ things during sessions, but as Carmen guided me through each light code, I was able to clearly visualise different images. For example, I saw green leaves and a lotus flower surrounding me; a huge pillar enveloping me, giving me a sense of protection; a beautiful, pearl-coloured sphere around me; and during the final light code, I saw black clouds around my throat and focused on releasing them from my body. It was such a powerful experience, and I felt a deep sense of healing and renewal.
